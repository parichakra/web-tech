Eyes

When there are no words, let your eyes do the talking

Introduction

Eyes are the chief organs of vision in human body and hence termed as photoreceptors.

The paired eyes in man are located inside bony sockets of skull called orbits

An eye is almost a spherical ball, guarded by two eye lids: upper eye lid and lower eye lid which can move frequently.
